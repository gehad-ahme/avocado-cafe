ABOUT US

Avocado Cafe was founded with an aim to become the best casual cafe in New York City. We began cooperating with the best vendors and purchasing the finest ingredients to prepare delicious food and beverages for our customers. We have created a cozy home-like atmosphere at our cafe so that our visitors could feel relaxed and carefree.
